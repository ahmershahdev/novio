How It Works

Three easy steps to start earning with Novio.

1. Sign Up

Apply for our affiliate program. Approval is fast, free, and open to creators of all sizes — bloggers, YouTubers, educators, and communities.

2. Share

Promote Novio courses using your unique tracking links. Embed them in videos, blog posts, social media, newsletters — anywhere your audience is.

3. Earn

Earn up to 30% commission on every successful referral. Get paid monthly via PayPal or bank transfer — no minimum threshold.
