Why Novio?

Build Something That Matters

At Novio, you won't just build software — you'll shape the way millions of people learn, grow, and launch new careers. We believe every line of code you write has the power to change someone's life.

Remote-First Culture

Work from anywhere in the world. Our distributed team spans 15+ countries and every timezone. Flexible hours that fit your lifestyle.

Fast Growth & Impact

Ship features that reach millions of learners. You'll own projects end-to-end and see the direct impact of your work within weeks — not quarters.

Never Stop Learning

Free access to every Novio course, an annual $2,500 learning budget, conference stipends, and dedicated time for personal growth.

Health & Wellness

Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance. Mental health support through therapy stipends and wellness Fridays off every month.

Competitive Compensation

Top-of-market salary, meaningful equity, annual bonuses, and a 401(k) match. We believe great work deserves great pay.

Inclusive & Diverse

A team that reflects the learners we serve. ERGs, mentorship programs, and a culture where every voice is heard and respected.

Our Process

How We Hire

Transparent, respectful, and fast. We aim to complete the process in under 2 weeks.

1
Apply

Submit your application. We review every single one — no black holes here.

2
Intro Call

A 30-minute chat with our recruiter to learn about each other and align on expectations.

3
Challenge

A take-home or live exercise relevant to the role, designed to respect your time (under 3 hours).

4
Offer

Meet the team, get your offer, and start making an impact from day one.
